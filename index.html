Cricket Field Editor

Paste a field setup — or an array of them — as JSON. Fine-tune by dragging, then share as images or print to take with you.

1

Generate a field

Copy the prompt, paste it into ChatGPT or Claude, and describe the field you want. It returns ready-to-paste JSON.

2

Paste & apply

Paste the JSON below and apply it to draw the field.

Paste one field setup, or an array of them for multiple diagrams. Positions use cricket names (e.g. 1st slip, gully, cover, mid-on, deep square leg); common shorthand and either spelling of mid-on / mid on are understood.

3

Tweak the diagram

Drag any fielder to reposition them, and click a diagram's title to rename it. Toggle what's shown:

Handedness (RH/LH) is set per diagram from each JSON's handedness.
